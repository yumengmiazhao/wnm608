Recipe

Meatball Soup

Ingredients

  • 300g Pork stuffing
  • 1 egg
  • 30g chopped green onion
  • 3g salt
  • 2g pepper

Instructions

  1. Put the ingredients together
  2. Stir in one direction (clockwise or counterclockwise) until the meat filling becomes thick. It usually takes about 20 minutes to stir.
  3. Use a small spoon to turn the meat filling into a ball
  4. After the water is boiled, put them into the pot, and take them out after the meatballs are floating.
  5. After the vegetables are cooked, put them into a bowl together with the meatballs, and the meatball soup is ready.

Enjoy your meatball soup!
